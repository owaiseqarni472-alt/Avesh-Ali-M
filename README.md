# Insurance Business & Executive Performance Dashboard

 Project Overview
This project analyzes insurance business performance and executive productivity using SQL, Excel, Tableau, and Power BI. The goal is to derive actionable insights on revenue trends, sales pipeline efficiency, renewal performance, and individual contributions through interactive dashboards.

---

  # Objectives
- Analyze overall business performance and revenue trends  
- Evaluate sales pipeline stages and conversion rates  
- Measure renewal vs new business contribution  
- Assess executive performance and productivity  
- Identify key areas for business improvement  

---

# Tools & Technologies
- **SQL** – Data extraction and querying  
- **Excel** – Data cleaning, transformation, and initial analysis  
- **Tableau** – Interactive dashboard (Business & Executive performance)  
- **Power BI** – Data visualization and reporting  

---

 
  ##Dashboards

# Business Performance Dashboard
- KPI metrics (Revenue, Target, Achievement %, Renewal %)  
- Sales pipeline analysis  
- Renewal vs Inception breakdown  
- Product-wise revenue distribution  
- Monthly and yearly trends  

# Executive Performance Dashboard
- Executive-wise revenue contribution  
- Target vs Actual performance  
- Achievement % comparison  
- Performance trend analysis  

---

# Key Insights
- Business achieved **63% of target**, indicating growth potential  
- Revenue is highly dependent on **new business (Inception)**  
- **Low renewal rate (~18%)** highlights retention challenges  
- Sales pipeline shows **high drop-off**, requiring better conversion strategy  
- Revenue contribution is concentrated among **top-performing executives**
